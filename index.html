Hao Peng

彭浩

I am a Professor at the School of Cyber Science and Technology, Beihang University.

I aim to make artificial intelligence technology easier and more accessible in industry. My broad research interests include structural data analysis, abnormal detection, text mining, multi-agent games, and industrial data mining and systems. My work often uses deep learning and reinforcement learning as design methodology.
